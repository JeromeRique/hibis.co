About Us
Green Market Santa Cruz is an ongoing experiment in community-based sustainable development.

About the App

This project is an initiative of the collaboration between the University of the West Indies, Department of Computing and Internet Technology and Green Market Santa Cruz.

Technologies Used

The Ionic framework was used to develop this Hybrid Application.

The goal of the project is to provide a high quality user interface for customers and vendors of The Green Market to manage and keep up with changes within the market's environment, and promote consumer interaction.

About the Team

A small team of four (4) within UWI DCIT undertook this project in a matter of two (2) weeks.
